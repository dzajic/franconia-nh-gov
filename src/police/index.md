The Franconia Police Department is proud to serve the community of Franconia by building trusting and cohesive community relationships through transparency, accountability, participation and engagement in our Town.&nbsp; Through our best discretion we will uphold the laws of the State of New Hampshire with honesty and integrity so that our citizens live in a safe, prosperous, culturally rich community and in doing so we will protect and improve the quality of all of our lives.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  
&nbsp;  
Vision Statement:
- We strive to build trust by taking every opportunity to treat people with kindness, dignity and respect.
- We will work to build cohesiveness in our community by reaching out to individuals, organizations, and businesses; to find ways to partner for the betterment of Franconia.
- We will be transparent by sharing information when we ethically can and as legally permissible.&nbsp; We will work hard to show the reasoning behind our thoughts and actions so that the gap between residents and the department is small and easily crossed.
- We will participate in our town by volunteering our time in and out of uniform and by taking part in its many exciting events.&nbsp;
- We will be honest in our words and deeds, through our consistent honesty we will display integrity.
- We will put ourselves in harm's way in order to protect our citizens and visitors from that which would hurt them, and we will work to create a safe and harmonious community in which to live, grow and raise a family.&nbsp;&nbsp;
