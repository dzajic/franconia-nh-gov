**  
​Please Note..  
Beginning July 15th, 2023 the Tri-town Transfer Station will only be accepting #1 & #2 plastic bottles for recycling. The Transfer Station is NOT accepting some #1 & #2 plastics to include "clam shells", which typically are used for salads or deli foods, oil containers, fuel additive containers, pesticide containers and plant pots. Please bring all **CLEAN** plastic & glass pre-sorted.**  
  

**COMMERCIAL/CONTRACTOR CONSTRUCTION DEBRIS AND BRUSH WILL NO LONGER BE ACCEPTED.**  
ONLY Residential Construction Debris and Brush will be accepted.  
Transfer Station Attendant MUST verify name and address of resident/homeowner.  
**Per order of Easton, Sugar Hill and Franconia Board of Selectmen.**
