**Rescue 2** carries extrication equipment (jaws of life), cutters & spreaders, cribbing.&nbsp; Scene lighting is provided with an on board generator. Water rescue equipment, ropes, self contained breathing apparatus, rear & front mounting winch, AED (heart monitor/defib),&nbsp; CO detector and multiple hand tools are all on board.  
  
**Engine 2** -&nbsp;1000 g.p.m. Pumper with 1000 gallons of water on board. Seats 5,&nbsp;with 3&nbsp;Self Contained Breathing Apparatus attached.&nbsp;150' of&nbsp;1-3/4" pre-connected&nbsp;attack line on front bumper, 200' of 1-3/4"&nbsp;cross lays&nbsp; behind the cab, a deck gun&nbsp;and 200' of 3" pre-connect to a blitz-gun attached to the rear. It also carries&nbsp; 1000' of 4" supply hose, chimney fire equipment, thermo image camera and CO detector, 2-24' extension ladders,12' closet ladder, gasoline powered&nbsp;hydraulic&nbsp; and water pumps&nbsp;and&nbsp;Scene lighting .  
  
**Engine&nbsp;1** - 1200 g.p.m. pumper with 2500 gallons of water.&nbsp; Seats 2, and has 4 self&nbsp; contained breathing apparatus on board.&nbsp; 200' of 1-3/4" pre-connect cross lay, 200' of 2-1/2" attack line, 200' of 3" pre-connect to a blitz-gun and 1200' of 4" supply hose.  
  
**Ladder 48** -&nbsp;&nbsp;105ft aerial with a 1500gpm pump, 500 gallon tank. Has a large selection of ground ladders as well as a set of hydraulic extrication tools. An onboard diesel generator to power scene lighting and cord reels for remote power.&nbsp; Seats 8 people and also carries additional technical rescue equipment to supplement rescue 2.&nbsp;  
  
**Mutual Aid** - Franconia Fire Department is part of Twin State Mutual Aid. On a first alarm, Sugar Hill, Easton and Bethlehem are on automatic.  
  
**Life Squad** - is dispatched for all emergency calls.
