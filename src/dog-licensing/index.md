## Dog Owners must register their dogs&nbsp;
 ![Picture](http://www.franconianh.org/uploads/1/1/6/8/11680191/7495363_orig.jpg)

  
  
**​License Dogs by April 30, 2024**  
  
**If&nbsp; dog is over 3 months old:  
Male/Female&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;$10.00  
Neutered/Spayed&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;$&nbsp; 7.50**  
**Owner is over 65&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; $&nbsp; 2.00  
Puppies (3-7 months)&nbsp; &nbsp; $&nbsp; 7.50**  
&nbsp; **&nbsp;**  
  
  
  
  
**Failure to comply will make you liable to a penalty of $25.00,&nbsp; if not licensed by June 1, 2019  
RSA 466:13**  
  
&nbsp; **Be sure to bring your Rabies Certificate**
