## The Frost Place
 ![Picture](http://www.franconianh.org/uploads/1/1/6/8/11680191/8270805.jpg)

**Robert and Elinor Frost owned this house from 1915 to  
1920, and some of the poet's most enduring poems were written here. Since 1977 a contemporary poet has been awarded a residency each summer.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;**  
  
  
The Frost Place re-opens Memorial Day weekend.
