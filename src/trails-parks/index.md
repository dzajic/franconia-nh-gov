Franconia Area NEMBA is an organization of outdoor enthusiasts interested in the preservation of trails in Franconia, Easton, Sugar Hill, Bethlehem and Littleton. This organization will provide manpower and money to help establish and maintain these trails, as well as help educating landowners and users about issues such as; liability, erosion and responsible behavior.  
**We are very excited to be part of _[Borderlands!](https://www.bikeborderlands.com/?fbclid=IwAR13iJ5pEE9yS2402upxBPQbJVyKKE1QY-VJa2aCphdlCsycEqr2HaUJFoo)_ Check out the new collaborative.**  
  
All interested individuals should become involved; come to our meetings, adopt a trail, join _[Franconia Area - NEMBA](https://www.nemba.org/chapters/fanemba)_. To find out more information email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi-l-email-protection/)
