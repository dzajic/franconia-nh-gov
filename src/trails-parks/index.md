Franconia Area NEMBA is an organization of outdoor enthusiasts interested in the preservation of trails in Franconia, Easton, Sugar Hill, Bethlehem and Littleton.&nbsp; This organization will provide manpower and money to help establish and maintain these trails, as well as help educating landowners and users about issues such as; liability, erosion and responsible behavior.  
**We are very excited to be part of [Borderlands!](http://www.bikeborderlands.com/) Check out the new collaborative.**  
  
All interested individuals should become involved; come to our meetings, adopt a trail, &nbsp;join [Franconia Area -&nbsp;NEMBA](http://www.nemba.org/chapters/fanemba). &nbsp;To find out more information email [[email&nbsp;protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection/)&nbsp;&nbsp;
