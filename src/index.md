## ** ATTENTION FRANCONIA RESIDENTS**  
  
THE FILING PERIOD FOR CANDIDATES FOR TOWN OFFICE IS JANUARY 21st THROUGH JANUARY 30th, 2026.  
  
VACANCIES EXIST IN THE FOLLOWING OFFICES:  
SELECTMAN (1)...................................... 3-year term  
TREASURER............................................. 1-year term  
TOWN MODERATOR........................... 2-year term  
TRUSTEE OF THE TRUST FUND (1)..3-year term  
SUPERVISOR OF THE CHECKLIST... 6-year term  
SUPERVISOR OF THE CHECKLIST... 2-year term  
LIBRARY TRUSTEE (2).......................... 3-year term  
CEMETERY TRUSTEE (1)..................... 3-year term  
WATER COMMISSIONER (1)............. 3-year term  
  
The Town Clerk’s Office will be open on Friday, January 30th, from 3-5pm to accept applications for candidacy **_only_** or during regular Town Clerk Hours: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday 8:00-4:00, and Friday 8:00-1:00
